Optimising Inventory & Strategizing Expansion of Corindo Spices

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the business operations of a wholesale spices company "Corindo Spices".
The goal is to identify key business problems related to pricing, inventory management, and regional expansion, 
and to provide data-driven insights and recommendations to support strategic decision-making.

Problem Statements

Challenges in Inventory Management: The company faces significant hurdles in managing inventory effectively, particularly during the launch of new products.
Market Position and Expansion Goals: With a solid foothold in West Bengal, Corindo Spices aims to broaden its market reach by penetrating new districts.

Objectives

- Analyze sales and stock data of spices
- Identify patterns in demand and pricing sensitivity
- Evaluate inventory performance across products
- Support expansion decisions using data insights
- Provide actionable business recommendations

Methodology

- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing (Python, Pandas, Excel)
- Predictive Modeling
- Quantitative Analysis Methods
- Visualization for insights

Tools & Technologies

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Excel, Google Colab

Key Insights

- Certain spices show consistently higher demand and better margins
- Pricing inconsistencies impact sales volume significantly
- New product launches require better demand forecasting
- Specific regions indicate strong potential for expansion
